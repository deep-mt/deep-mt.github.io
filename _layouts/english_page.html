About this project

This project advances the cutting-edge research and development of machine translation and deep learning so that computers can understand context, scene, and conversation.

1

Context-aware MT

  • Conversation translation
  • Translation depending on context
  • Translation with omitted information
  • Consistency of word usage


2

MT for news articles

  • Semi-automatic corpus acquisition for MT
  • Translating news articles
  • Translation with neologisms
  • Summarization / headline generation

3

Multi-modal MT

  • Context / scene understanding from image and language
  • Image scene recognition
  • Multi-lingual caption generation
